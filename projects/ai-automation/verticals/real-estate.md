# Vertical Deep-Dive: Real Estate Agencies

_Parent: [[verticals]]_

## Who exactly

### RU market
- **Small-to-mid agencies** in Moscow/SPB (5-30 agents)
- **Solo realtors with personal brand** (Instagram/TG followings)
- **Not:** big chains (Этажи, ИНКОМ) — too slow, committee buying

### Diaspora market — THE MONEY
- **RU-speaking realtors selling Dubai/Cyprus/Georgia/Turkey/Thailand property to RU speakers who relocated**
- This market EXPLODED post-2022 and is still growing
- Often solo operators or 2-5 person teams
- Already spend heavily on Instagram ads, understand marketing
- Paying in USD/EUR

## Pain landscape

Ranked by intensity:

1. **Lead qualification burns hours** — agent gets 50 inquiries/week, 45 are tire-kickers. "Сколько стоит квартира в Дубае?" with no budget/timeline/visa info.
2. **Follow-up fails** — after a viewing, 80% of leads go cold because nobody nurtures them.
3. **Content production** — listings need descriptions for 5 platforms, social posts, stories, reels.
4. **Instant response on Instagram/TG** — leads come at 11pm, agent replies next day, lead is gone.
5. **Matching property to client** — agent's inventory is in spreadsheets, matching to lead criteria is manual.
6. **Market update newsletters** — past clients go cold, no systematic outreach.
7. **Document handling** — contracts, visa applications, utility setup for diaspora buyers.

## Packaged offer options

### Offer A: "AI Lead Qualifier" ⭐ FLAGSHIP
**What:** TG/WhatsApp/Instagram bot that qualifies leads before agent touches them. Collects budget, location preference, timeline, visa status, financing source. Pushes qualified leads to CRM with summary.

- Setup: **$2,000-3,000**
- Monthly: **$400-600**
- Delivery: 10-14 days
- **Best fit:** realtors drowning in Instagram DMs

### Offer B: "Viewing-to-Close Nurture"
**What:** Automated multi-touch drip after property viewing. Personalized by property, lead profile, objections raised. Includes similar listings, price changes, area updates.

- Setup: **$1,500-2,000**
- Monthly: **$300**
- Delivery: 7-10 days
- **Best fit:** agents with consistent viewing pipeline

### Offer C: "Listing Content Engine"
**What:** From listing data → generates platform-specific descriptions (RU+EN+AR for Dubai), Instagram posts, stories scripts, reels hooks.

- Setup: **$1,500**
- Monthly: **$250-400**
- Delivery: 7 days
- **Best fit:** content-hungry agents, entry offer

## Risks

### Market
- **Realtors are DIY-inclined** — "I'll build it myself with ChatGPT"
- **Commission-based income = irregular spending** — tight months, they cut tools first
- **Market crashes hit first** — Dubai real estate cools, realtors stop buying tools
- **Regulation in RU** — real estate law changes, need to adapt scripts

### Client
- **Lead data quality varies** — some agents give you garbage leads to "qualify", system looks dumb
- **Visa/legal questions** — bot must NEVER answer these authoritatively (liability risk)
- **Agents resistant to process change** — want bot to "just work" without them updating
- **Multi-language support** (Dubai: RU+EN+AR) adds complexity

### Execution
- **Instagram bot restrictions** — Meta can shut down integrations
- **WhatsApp Business API** costs and compliance
- **Integration with CRM** (AmoCRM, Bitrix24, HubSpot, PropertyFinder) — each is different
- **Every realtor's sales funnel is slightly different** — resist customization, productize ruthlessly

## Decision criteria (when to commit / when to kill)

**Commit if:**
- Instagram DM outreach → 3+ calls in week 1
- At least 2 pilots close at $1.5k
- First client's lead quality improves measurably within 30 days

**Kill if:**
- Realtors won't share their lead flow data (bad sign for case study)
- Integration complexity blows up scope (each CRM = custom work, kills productization)
- Case study metrics are flat (AI didn't actually help → offer is broken)

## Kira's take

**This is the cleanest fit of the three for a productized service + YT funnel.**

Why:
- Diaspora realtors are the easiest RU-speaking buyer demographic to reach (Instagram is public)
- Their pain is quantifiable (leads in → deals out)
- Case studies have drama (revenue numbers, closed deals)
- Your YT content has a HUGE audience overlap (RU-speakers in diaspora watching AI content)
- No regulation headaches (unlike clinics)
- Less commoditized than e-commerce — fewer existing AI tools for RU-speaking realtors

**Best offer to lead with:** Offer A (AI Lead Qualifier). Biggest pain, biggest ROI story, best case study fuel.

**Flag:** integration with their CRM is the main execution risk. Lock down ONE CRM to support at launch (probably AmoCRM — most common in RU-speaking agency world).
