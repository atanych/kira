# Strategic Plan

_Last updated: 2026-04-18_

## Goal
- **Revenue target:** $15k/mo (medium-term, main income)
- **Time budget:** 20-30 hrs/week (while still tech lead at archive.com)
- **Exit condition:** replace archive.com income

## Business Model

**Productized service + YouTube funnel.**

### Stack
| Layer | Offer | Price | Role |
|---|---|---|---|
| Top of funnel | Russian-language YouTube channel about AI | Free | Authority + audience |
| Core revenue | Productized AI automation service | $1-4k setup | Main business |
| High-ticket upsell | Consulting/audit | $3-10k | Future, when brand strong |

### Rules
- **Async-first delivery** — no live calls during archive.com work hours. Loom + Telegram.
- **Setup + handoff model** — client owns it after. No ongoing babysitting.
- **Avoid real-time/customer-facing use cases** — those break on Saturday night.
- **Zero custom projects once productized** — fixed scope, fixed price, fixed timeline.

### Rejected models
- ❌ **SaaS** — 12-18mo to revenue, too slow, RU/BY recurring-billing rails broken
- ❌ **Pure consulting** — no authority yet, RU market wants deliverables not PowerPoints
- ❌ **Pure agency** — time-for-money trap, every project custom, doesn't scale

## Payment Rails

### Phase 1 — now until $5k/mo
- Invoice via Deel or direct SWIFT to BY card (already working for archive.com income)
- Operate as BY ИП or individual
- AI tool payments: already solved

### Phase 2 — Georgia ИП migration trigger
- Revenue hits $5k/mo consistently, OR
- A client refuses to pay to BY, OR
- Deel fees eat margin at scale

### Georgia ИП specs
- 1% tax up to ~$150k/year revenue (small business status)
- Setup: ~$300-500, 1 week, optional 1-day Tbilisi trip
- Unlocks: USD account (TBC/Bank of Georgia), Wise, Payoneer, clean SWIFT
